What is Dota?




You win a game of Dota by destroying the enemy "Ancient"

There are two teams: Radiant and Dire

Each team consists of 5 players

There are 11 towers that stand in the way of each teams' Ancient

The Ancient can't be destroyed until at least 5 of the towers are destroyed

Ancients

There are currently 115 heroes in Dota 2.

Players take control of these heroes to destroy the enemy Ancient!
